This program is for the individual who wants to explore their boundaries. Someone who values physical, mental, emotional and spiritual wellbeing as the conduit to a rich and fulfilling life.

Our wellness program is customized to you, your current health and your goals. We have helped hundreds of high performing people live their signature life.

Live It Concierge Wellness is comprehensive. It combines physical training, exercise physiology, behavioral science, nutrition, mindfulness and so much more.

We analyze your concierge medical recommendations, perform baseline testing, and listen to your needs and goals. We develop a process and guide you towards a fitter, happier morevibrant life.

Supportive services include one-on-one live coaching, daily rituals, nutrition counseling, physical therapy and mindfulness training.

What you can expect: Increased strength and stamina. Improved body composition. More energy and productivity at work and play. A sense of presence and connection in your personal relationships. Overall enhanced sense of well-being.

Serious inquiries only.

Explore the Concierge Program
This program is for highly motivated athletic women. Women who want to feel at home in their bodies, at peace in their minds and ready to engage in everything life has to offer.

These are Live on-line group training sessions taught in real-time. Form and alignment adjustments are provided at a level typically only found in one-on-one personal training sessions.

There are four live group sessions per week. These incorporate our signature Lean, Strong and Loose protocols so you are well rounded as you transform into a leaner, stronger, more flexible athletic women.

What you can expect: A leaner, stronger more athletic physique. A newfound understanding and appreciation for your body and how it moves. A deeper connection to self, feeling more grounded and able to navigate the complexities of life. A sense of camaraderie and achievement with other like-minded women.

This program is only available to 40 women. Please contact Live It Training if you would like to reserve a spot.
