Our Coaches

Rod Connolly

Rod Connolly

Live It Training founder and exercise physiologist, Rod Connolly brings 27 years of coaching experience. Rod works with professional and Olympic athletes, celebrities and titans of industry. His unique and extensive background combines: athletic training, martial arts, elite cycling coaching, yoga and contemplative sciences.
  • EDUCATION
    Master of Science - Exercise Physiology
  • CLASSES
    Lean, Strong, Loose
  • STRENGTHS
    Mindfulness, Athletic mobility, Clinical Exercise
  • PASSIONS
    Cycling, Reading, Travel, Meditation, Yoga

My Philosophy

Humans were meant to move. We engage in life in a deeper way when we move our bodies. We unlock something in ourselves when we honor our physical nature. You not only connect with your inner resources, you connect better with others. I will support and teach you how to move your body in a more efficient, athletic way. I am merely the coach. You are the athlete.

I feel alive when...

I help someone recognize and take action on their potential.


Which artist is always on your playlist?

Some days I rock; some days I rave; some days I rap. Rock - Radiohead, Pearl Jam, U2, Jane’s Addiction, Led Zeppelin. Rave - Satori, Solomon, Maceo Plex. Rap - Notorious BIG, Tupac and Tribe Called Quest.


What health and wellness related topics do you know more about than anyone would ever need to know?

Evolutionary biology and anthropology. I get super nerded out on these topics. When we recognize our origins it helps understand the why and how behind teaching movement patterns.


What motivates you to keep improving and growing?

Life. It is miraculous we are born into this world. If you are reading this, you likely are born into a rather fortunate situation compared to many in the world. I aim to honor the gift of life by learning and evolving. I want to develop myself and help others cultivate their gifts.


A book that changed your life?

East of Eden. John Steinbeck blows my mind every time I read him. Not only in the exquisite writing and story lines, but he was such a progressive thinker. He is on my short list if you could invite anyone living or deceased to a dinner party.


How has a coach for Live It Training changed the way you approach life?

Coaching to a group versus one - on - one has helped me understand the power of community. I am naturally a bit of an introvert. Seeing the camaraderie, the support and energy that takes place with a group striving to better themselves and support one another is powerful. It is something I have embraced and love seeing the students exceed their expectations.


In your coaching, What corrector; or feedback can you not stress enough?

Develop skill instead of jumping into speed or just getting reps done. Bring focus and intention to your movements. Not only will you experience increased benefits, but your incidence or injury or joint pain will subside dramatically.
