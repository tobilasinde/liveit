Mike Testwuide

Motivation, energy, community. This is what you will experience in every one of Mike’s sessions. Mike brings his background of captaining teams at the professional and Olympic level to help you dig deep. He will challenge you and support you. The best parts of you will flourish with his inspired coaching.

My Philosophy

When you put together all the ingredients for wholistic fitness, you can break through preconceived barriers and reach your true potential. I have over 20 years of experience self-experimenting and diving deep into the science of optimizing human performance. This is my calling. To share this with our students.

I feel alive when...

I am engaged with motivated people. I thrive in environments that challenge and bring out the best in you.


Which artist is always on your playlist?

Avicii.


What health and wellness related topics do you know more about than anyone would ever need to know?

How to push myself to the limit both physically and mentally. And then honor recovery. My coaching culture helps my students go to the edge, and develop the insight to recognize the need for rest.


What motivates you to keep improving and growing?

The look on an athlete’s face after an intense training session. It’s an expression of accomplishment and owning their power. I am fueled by our students success stories.



A book that changed your life?

The Boys in the Boat by Daniel James Brown. Nine unique individuals set aside the hardships they endured during the Great Depression and come together to win Olympic gold. A true story that personifies the importance of hard work, dedication and resilience. These core qualities are consistent drivers in my life.


How has a coach for Live It Training changed the way you approach life?

Live It Training is the next extension of a life filled with teamwork and camaraderie. Live It Training is not just another fitness training regimen. it's a community of people who want to live their best life and see the value of doing hard work.


In your coaching, What corrector; or feedback can you not stress enough?

Focused, confident, and deliberate movements. When you do a session, do it 100%. Show up for yourself in a meaningful way.
