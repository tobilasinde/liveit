About Us

How We Came Alive

Your story is our story. We exist to help you come alive. Our team of coaches has a breadth of experience in physical and mental training. Our students thrive in the intentional, wholistic approach we bring to each session. This is a community rooted in a belief of living life as an adventure to be relished.

Why Did We Create
 Live It Training?

Because your success fuels us
We believe success is knowing yourself, being good with it, and having the courage to share it with the world.

Regardless of your beginnings, you have the ability to live your signature life. To come fully alive and shine your light.

Our calling is to help you engage in a lifestyle to dive deeper into who you are and your true capabilities.

We are here to challenge and support you physically, mentally and emotionally. We have witnessed thousands of transformations of people just like you.

Have the courage to take the first step with us. We will support you on the next.

OUR CREDS

Live It Training is designed by our founder and exercise physiologist, Rod Connolly. Rod has 28 years of experience coaching professional and Olympic athletes and titans of business. Rod brings an extensive and unique background from athletic training, martial arts, elite cycling coaching, contemplative sciences and yoga.

The Live It Training coaching team brings a wealth of experience as Certified Athletic Trainers, Movement Practitioners, Olympic athletes and Clinical Exercise Specialists.

We teach. You will develop skill and technique so you can progress and mitigate injury. You will understand how and why to train with intent. Come alive with us and live a life full of energy, vitality and adventure.
